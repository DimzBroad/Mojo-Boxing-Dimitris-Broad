ABOUT MOJO BOXING

Find your mojo with Mojo Boxing & Fitness, a fitness club located in the heart of Sea Point, Cape Town.

Run by Joseph Chinana, the Mojo Boxing & Fitness team combine fitness and boxing in high energy, high intensity classes where you will be pushed constantly.
All you have to do is show up and Mojo Boxing & Fitness will take care of the trest!

LET US HELP YOU REACH YOUR GOALS

Each session is unique thereby literally keeping you on your toes and ready to roll with the punches.

The classes offer high intensity exercise, including boxing, cardio, weights and sparing – all tailored for a full body workout geared to help you reach your fitness goals. Whether you are looking to lose weight, tone up, hone your boxing skills, or simply release extra energy, Mojo Boxing & Fitness will help you get strong and fit in a motivating and friendly environment where you will find yourself being pushing to your limits in every session.
